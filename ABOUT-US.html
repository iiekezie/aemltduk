Mission Statement

Advanced Earth Metals Ltd are committed to providing construction services that surpass our Clients’ expectations. We will accomplish this through the selective use of reputable sub-contractors, hiring the best construction minds and retaining first class employees. We have always approached the building process with honesty, integrity and a great deal of skill thus providing the foundation for long term customer relations and growth. We are committed to the success of our Clients, our business partners, and our employees.

Vision Statement


Advanced Earth Metals Ltd is united in its vision to create and maintain an organization of highly skilled construction professionals that serve our Clients’ needs with honesty, integrity and skill. To provide an environment that promotes personal growth and self-pride and undertake projects that make a big difference to people worldwide. Ultimately, to differentiate ourselves as a company of choice for clients seeking a company who can deliver on time, quality, personal service, and value.

Code of Business Ethics

  Core values of Advanced Earth Metals Ltd . are based on serving the country and people, therefore we strive to realize our dedication to these goals and values in action, and in professional relations by adhering to the following principles:

  • Creating value and national self-belief in line with promoting Kayson’s brand.
  • Collaborating toward excellence and increasing welfare in our surrounding community (employees and their families, projects stakeholders, society and people).
  • Avoiding any act against public interest.
  • Providing a healthy, peaceful and secure workplace for personnel while promoting healthy relationships with all stakeholders.
  • Sustainable natural resources utilizing, protecting the environment, observing safety and occupational health in our activities.
  • Observing relevant national and international laws and regulations.
  • Maintaining fairness, justice, honesty and transparency in relations.
  • Taking responsibility, guaranteeing fulfillment of job commitments, adhering to plans and schedules and improving quality and disciplines.
  • Considering interests of stakeholders and general public in a free, healthy and competitive environment.
  • Observing information confidentiality.
  • Respecting intellectual properties of others.
  • Avoiding leverage, accepting or offering any advantages or gifts which may cause conflicts of interests or rights violations.
  • Respecting multiculturalism and diversity.
  • Providing equal opportunities based on competency; regardless of age, gender or race.
  • Valuing teamwork and preferring organizational interests to individual’s.
  • Admitting probable flaws and striving to resolve the consequences.
  • Preserving and using tangible and intangible assets and resources of the company effectively.

The essence of all above which has to be observed is the win-win-win relationship; which means not only both parties of a business deal should consider each other’s interests but also their agreement must preserve the interests of the country, people and other parties.

OUR HISTORY
Advanced Earth Metals Ltd is a privately-owned construction group that formed in 2021 bringing together a group of skilled and successful companies under one brand and a shared set of values to form a business that is capable of operating at a multi-disciplinary capacity. Since its formation the business has undertaken various projects worldwide and has undertaken projects in civil, commercial and residential for a wide array of clients from private enterprises to national and local governments.

Safety
Safety is an integral part of everything we do.

Advanced Earth Metals Ltd is determined to provide and maintain a safe workplace for all staff, subcontractors, clients, suppliers and visitors. We work closely with clients to understand their health and safety requirements, in order to ensure compliance with their safety systems and provide safe project management teams.

At Advanced Earth Metals Ltd we believe all incidents can be prevented by fostering a strong safety culture and integrating safety within all our activities. We understand that a safe work environment means employees must be fit for work and able to perform their work tasks in a secure, productive and effective manner.
